# VAIDYASETU PREDICTIVE MODELS & ALGORITHMS

**1. IDRS (Indian Diabetes Risk Score) Regression**
Used for: Diabetes and Pre-Diabetes

**2. Bayesian Likelihood Ratio Model**
Used for: Thyroid Disorders

**3. Weighted Rule-Based Regression (ICMR-Aligned)**
Used for: Hypertension and Heart Disease

**4. Multi-Factor Risk Weighting**
Used for: Anemia and PCOD

**5. LLM-Augmented Predictive Refinement (Llama-3.3)**
Used for: Final Risk Score Adjustment

**6. Linear Mitigation Reduction Algorithm**
Used for: Post-Assessment Risk Mitigation

**7. Population Baseline Normalization**
Used for: Indian-specific Prevalence Calibration
