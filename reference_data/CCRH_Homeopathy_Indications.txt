Common Homeopathic Remedies & Interactions with Allopathy

1. ARNICA MONTANA
   - Indications: Bruising, muscle soreness, post-operative recovery.
   - Interaction: Potentiates Antiplatelet/Anticoagulant drugs (Aspirin, Warfarin).
   - Risk: Increased bleeding tendency.

2. BELLADONNA
   - Indications: Acute inflammation, sudden high fever.
   - Interaction: Anticholinergic drugs (Atropine).
   - Risk: Additive side effects (dry mouth, blurred vision).

3. HYPERICUM PERFORATUM
   - Indications: Nerve injuries, shooting pains.
   - Interaction: SSRIs (Fluoxetine, Sertraline).
   - Risk: Serotonin syndrome (mild additive effect).

4. GELSEMIUM
   - Indications: Weakness, dizziness, anxiety.
   - Interaction: CNS Depressants.
   - Risk: Increased drowsiness.

Note: Homeopathic remedies are highly diluted, but "mother tinctures" or low potencies (3C, 6C) can have significant physiological interactions.
