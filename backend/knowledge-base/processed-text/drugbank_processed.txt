Source: DrugBank
Record: Warfarin
Content:
Drug Profile for Warfarin:
Mechanism of Action: Warfarin competitively inhibits the vitamin K epoxide reductase complex 1 (VKORC1), an essential enzyme for activating the vitamin K available in the body.
Known Drug Interactions:
- Interacts with Acetaminophen: Acetaminophen may increase the anticoagulant activities of Warfarin.


--- DRUGBANK RECORD ---
Source: DrugBank
Record: Acetaminophen
Content:
Drug Profile for Acetaminophen:
Mechanism of Action: Acetaminophen exerts its effects through inhibition of the cyclooxygenase (COX) pathways.
Known Drug Interactions:
- Interacts with Warfarin: The risk or severity of bleeding can be increased when Acetaminophen is combined with Warfarin.
