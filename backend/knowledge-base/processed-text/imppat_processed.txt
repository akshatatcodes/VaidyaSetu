Record: Withania somnifera
Content:
Indian Medicinal Plant 'Indian Ginseng' (Botanical: Withania somnifera, Sanskrit: Ashwagandha) is traditionally used for Stress, Anxiety, Inflammation, Immunomodulation. It contains active phytochemicals such as Withaferin A, Withanolide D, Sitoindoside IX, which interact with molecular targets including COX-2, TNF-alpha, NF-kappaB.
