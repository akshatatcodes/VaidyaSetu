# 📚 VaidyaSetu Data Sources & Bookmarks

This file serves as the official bookmark collection for the project research.

## 💊 Core Pharmacology & Interaction Databases
*   **IMPPAT:** [https://www.imppat.com/](https://www.imppat.com/) (Indian Medicinal Plants, Phytochemicals and Therapeutics)
*   **DrugBank:** [https://go.drugbank.com/](https://go.drugbank.com/) (Comprehensive drug data and interactions)
*   **PubChem:** [https://pubchem.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/](https://pubchem.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/) (Chemical structures and molecular pathways)

## 🩺 Indian Standards & Epidemiology
*   **ICMR:** [https://www.icmr.gov.in/](https://www.icmr.gov.in/) (Indian Council of Medical Research)
*   **AYUSH Ministry:** [https://ayush.gov.in/](https://ayush.gov.in/) (Official standards for Ayurveda, Yoga, Unani, Siddha, and Homoeopathy)

## 🌿 Traditional Knowledge & Pharmacovigilance
*   **TKDL:** [http://www.tkdl.res.in/](http://www.tkdl.res.in/) (Traditional Knowledge Digital Library)
*   **CCRH:** [https://www.ccrhindia.nic.in/](https://www.ccrhindia.nic.in/) (Central Council for Research in Homoeopathy)
*   **DRAVYA Portal:** [https://dravya.ayush.gov.in/](https://dravya.ayush.gov.in/) (Standardization of Ayurvedic and Unani ingredients)
*   **Ayush Suraksha Portal:** [https://ayushsuraksha.gov.in/](https://ayushsuraksha.gov.in/) (Pharmacovigilance for AYUSH drugs)
